The Moroccan tagine, a culinary treasure !

Tajine is a kind of stew cooked in a stew that can be a mixture of meat, poultry or fish, vegetables or fruit and spices and olive oil. Its origin is a mystery. Its etymology would come from the Greek Teganene which means "earthen dish".
